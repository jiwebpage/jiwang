Welcome

I am currently an associate professor in the Department of Automation at Xiamen University, China. I received the Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2018 from Chongqing University, China. From 2019 to 2021, I was a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at University of California, San Diego, La Jolla, CA, USA. I has coauthored (with M. Krstic) the book entitled PDE Control of String-Actuated Motion (Princeton University Press). My research Interests are control and estimation of distributed parameter systems, adaptive control, safe control, and applications in flexible robots.
